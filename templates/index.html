About Qard al-Hasanah

Qard al-Hasanah is an Islamic concept of interest-free loans given or taken for the sake of Allah. It is a form of "good loan" where the lender does not charge any interest or additional amount over the money lent.

Our system helps manage these interest-free loans by tracking loan applications, approvals, installment schedules, and payments in a transparent and efficient manner. Whether you are an administrator overseeing the loans or a borrower tracking your payments, our platform provides all the tools you need.
